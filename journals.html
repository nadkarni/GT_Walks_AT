  • November 8th, 2014

    Up and Ready!

    Savannah Lee, Mahalakshmi Srinivasan, Rahul Raina, Will Su and Professor Crawford set out to Hawk Mountain to begin their hiking journey. They do not know what to expect for the next two days and are packed and ready to walk.

  • November 9th, 2014

    Summit.

    The day starts of strong, yet tiring. The group reaches the beautiful peak of Springer Mountain around late afternoon, and together they enjoy their delicious mountain houses (well most of them do).

  • November 10th, 2014

    The Final Hours

    GT walks AT finally reaches Amicalola Falls after a little over 8 mile hike down from Springer Mountain. Due to particular issues (losing the keys), Professor Crawford hikes back to Hawk Mountain, leaving the students to hike by themselves. Quickly, yet carefully, the students make it back safely.
